Faculty Of Engineering In Shubra Benha University

The origins of the faculty trace back to the Engineering Division of the Higher Technical Institute in Cairo, which was established in October 1975. Later, it was merged into the Faculty of Technology in Mataria-Helwan University.

In April 1976, it was transferred to Ain Shams University under the name "Faculty of Engineering".

On October 30, 1976, it became affiliated with Zagazig University, Benha Branch.

Finally, in 2005, it became one of the key colleges of Benha University.
